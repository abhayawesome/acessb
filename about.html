About Ace SSB

Your Trusted Partner on the Path to Becoming an Officer

Our Story

Founded in 2014, Ace SSB was born from a singular vision: to provide every aspiring candidate with the highest quality, most effective preparation materials for the Services Selection Board (SSB) interview. What started as a small initiative to help fellow aspirants has grown into a trusted resource for candidates across the nation, guiding them on their journey to join the armed forces.

Our Expert Team

Ace SSB is powered by a team of experts with deep experience in the SSB selection process. Our specialists meticulously craft all our preparation materials, including our renowned PPDT, TAT, WAT, SRT, and GTO series, ensuring they are relevant, challenging, and reflective of the actual SSB tests.

Proven Success

We take immense pride in the success of our candidates. Many aspirants who have utilized our materials have gone on to not only qualify but also secure recommendations from various SSBs. Their success is a testament to the quality and effectiveness of our resources and strengthens our resolve to continue our mission.
